ESL One, OMEGA League, OGA Dota PIT, BTS Pro Series, and more

The Esports Kingdom team (@KingOfEsports)

2020.10.18

There have been a bunch of events in the DOTA2 esports space including ESL One Thailand, the OMEGA League, OGA Dota PIT Season 3, and the BTS Pro Series. Where do all of the teams stack up now?

PLEASE NOTE: Different regions tiers are not comparable - teams that are in the Europe A-Tier are not necessarily equal to those in the SEA A-Tier. Each region has been ranked individually and isolated from the others. We have also temporarily removed the S-Tier, reserving that for when the regions collide again and a 'King of Dota2' can truly be named.
