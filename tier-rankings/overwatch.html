20 May 2021 (2021 May Melee)

The first mini-tournament of the 2021 Overwatch League season has come to a conclusion with a surprising Dallas Fuel win propelling them to the top of our Tier Rankings alongside two other teams while we see many squads drop down the tiers.

15 April 2021 (2021 Pre-Season)

The 2021 Season for the Overwatch League is just about to kick off so Zekzal takes a look at how the teams stack up before the action begins in a special pre-season edition of the Overwatch Tier Rankings.

18 October 2020 (Overwatch League Playoffs)

The Overwatch League for 2020 wrapped up recently with a grand champion across the regions being crowned. We take a look at the entire season and place the teams on our tier rankings for the last time in 2020!

16 August 2020 (Overwatch League Countdown (Weeks 24-27))

The Countdown Cup has come to end and once again the Shanghai Dragons and San Francisco Shock rise up to the top spots in their respective region - but how do the rest of the teams stack up in the tier rankings?

12 July 2020 (Overwatch League Summer (Weeks 19-22))

Summer Showdown came down to a exciting finish with two new teams in the Paris Eternal and Guangzhou Charge picking up the victories for their regions. What has this done for the overall landscape of our Overwatch League tier rankings?

27 May 2020 (Overwatch League May (Weeks 12-16))

May Melee has finished with San Francisco Shock winning the North American competition and the Shanghai Dragons winning the Asian competition. That means it is time for us to get out our pens and paper to assess the Overwatch League teams for another edition of the tier rankings.

26 April 2020 (Overwatch League Weeks 1-11)

We have had tier rankings for Counter-Strike, League of Legends, and Rainbow Six, now it is time to branch into Overwatch. In this first edition of the Overwatch Tier Rankings we place all of the OWL teams (and some Contenders teams) into S, A, B, C, or D-Tier based on their current skill-level.
