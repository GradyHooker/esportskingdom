8 March 2021 (2021 Stage One)

The first stage of the 2021 season of the Call of Duty League has been completed and competition is heating up at the top. Stage Two will be an interesting one to see which teams can keep their grip on the top and which fall down a tier or two.

26 January 2021 (2021 Pre-Season KickOff)

The 2021 Season for the Call of Duty League has kicked off! Officially the results form this week don't factor into the season rankings, but we used it as a chance to see how the teams stack up in the pre-season.

2 September 2020 (CoD League Championship)

Congratulations to Dallas Empire for taking out the 2020 Call of Duty League Championship! That brings the 2020 season to a close and as such we have one final tier rankings to see where the teams ended up, featuring some surprising down-ranks.

29 July 2020 (CoD League Weeks 11-13)

The Regular Season is completed as now we know how the CoD League teams will be seeded going into the Playoffs. Two teams take the top spot in S-Tier as well as the top seeding right into the Quarterfinals.

23 June 2020 (CoD League Weeks 9 & 10)

We had another two weeks of Call of Duty League competition and it is time for another edition of our Tier Rankings. We see a new team leap into S-Tier while multiple other previously-strong teams slip downwards towards the bottom of the rankings.

25 May 2020 (CoD League Weeks 1-8)

As the Call of Duty League ramps up it felt like a good time to launch of fifth game in our Tier Rankings series. We have enlisted the help of Coy DePratt to help our usual suspect Grady to place the 12 teams into 5 tiers based on their recent performance.
