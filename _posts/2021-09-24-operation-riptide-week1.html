"Shark Week" - The new operation kicks off with a bang and a set of missions for you to complete and a maximum of 10 stars to earn towards your operation goals.

Operation Riptide Week 1 Challenges:

[Food Chain] Deathmatch - Mirage (1 Star)

  • Get 10 Kills in a series of different radar locations

    • [Blood In The Water] Danger Zone - Blacksite (3 Stars)

  • Complete a variety of tasks (1 task, 3 tasks, 5 tasks)

    • Competitive - Short Match - Premier (3 Stars)

  • Win 1 Match OR
  • Win 21 Rounds

    • Wingman - Extraction (2 Stars)

  • Deal 300 Utility Damage OR
  • Deal 200 Utility Damage in a Single Match

    • [It's Raining Sharks] Guardian - Blacksite (2 Stars)

  • Survive an aerial assault

    • [Feeding Frenzy] Casual - Group Delta (2 Stars)

  • Get 30 Kills OR
  • Get 4 Multi-Kill Rounds in a Single Match

    • Good luck in your Operation Riptide challenges this week!
