Overwatch League Week 15 - Hero Bans and Match-ups

Grady Hooker (@GradyHooker)

2020.05.12

It is yet again another week in the OverWatch League and that means that we have a brand new set of Hero Bans and Match-ups for you. Come and see which heroes will not be appearing this week and what time your favorite teams will face off.

Hero Bans

Hero pools are an algorithm-driven way to figure out which heroes are being played the most and remove them from play for the next week. There is an element of randomness, but heroes with a higher play-rate will have a higher chance of being banned. There is one exception and that is if a hero was banned last week, they cannot be banned again the next week.

The four heroes that will be banned for Week 15 play are:

  • Damage: Mei & Tracer
  • Tank: Orisa
  • Support: Moira

    • For last week (Week 14) the banned heroes were Reaper & Ashe (Damage), Reinhardt (Tank), and Brigitte (Support).
