Rainbow Six Regional Tier Rankings:
Pro League Season 11

The Esports Kingdom team (@KingOfEsports)

2020.04.25

The current COVID-19 pandemic has forced changes in the esports world and as it gets pushed towards regional competitions, Esports Kingdom also has to adapt and move our tier rankings temporarily towards regional tier rankings. So come and see how each team stacks up in their regions!

PLEASE NOTE: Different regions tiers are not comparable - teams that are in the Europe A-Tier are not necessarily equal to those in the Asia-Pacific A-Tier. Each region has been ranked individually and isolated from the others. We have also temporarily removed the S-Tier, reserving that for when the regions collide again and a 'King of Rainbow Six' can truly be named.

Tier Rankings compiled by Konstantin "Cthulhu" Nuridzhanyan (@CthulhuANZ on Twitter) and Grady Hooker (@GradyHooker on Twitter).
