CSGO introduces a new case, 4 new weapon collections, and new stickers and patches

Grady Hooker (@GradyHooker)

2021.09.23

In the second part of today's Counter-Strike update, Valve added a bunch of new community content to the game. We have four brand new weapon collections (Train 2021, Mirage 2021, Dust2 2021, and Vertigo 2021) as well as 2 new sticker packs (Operation Riptide and Riptide Surf Shop) and a set of patches just for Operation Riptide.
