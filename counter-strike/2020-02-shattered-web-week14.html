Operation Shattered Web
Week 14 Challenges

Grady Hooker (@GradyHooker)

2020.02.19

"From Russia With Love" - It is time for you to pull out you PP-Bizon and your AK-47 to get some kills. You will be heading to Train a lot as well as dipping your toes into the Demolition gamemode for the first time in this operation.

Shattered Web Week 14 Challenges:

  • You Only Live Twice (1 Star): Get 8 round wins in Wingman: Train
  • Son of a Kalashnikov! (1 Star): Get 10 kills with the PP-Bizon in Casual: Defusal Group Delta
  • Gaining the Upper Hand (2 Stars): Win 5 or more rounds in a single half of a Competitive: Cache match
  • Space Race (2 Stars): Step on 3 bumpmines in Danger Zone
  • War of Attrition (3 Stars): Get 1 Desert Eagle kill in Demolition
  • Avtomat Kalashinkova, 1947 (3 Stars): Get 10 AK-47 kills in Guardian: Train

    • Good luck in your Shattered Web challenges this week!
