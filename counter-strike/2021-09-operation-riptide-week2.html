Operation Riptide
Week 2 Challenges

Grady Hooker (@GradyHooker)

2021.09.29

"Seal The Deal" - The second week of Operation Riptide is underway and that means a new set of challenges for you. This week we are trying out the new map Basalt, defending Sirocco from an Amphibious Assault, and playing some Deathmatch.

Operation Riptide Week 2 Challenges:

Competitive - Short Match - Basalt (3 Stars)

  • Win 1 Match OR
  • Win 21 Rounds

    • [Amphibious Assault] Guardian (2 Stars)

  • Get kills in Guardian: Sirocco

    • [Aim Above The Flippers] Deathmatch - Group Sigma (2 Stars)

  • Get 50 Headshot Kills OR
  • Get 30 Headshot Kills in a Single Match

    • [Explosive Swimmer] Demolition (1 Star)

  • Get 14 Kills OR
  • Deal Grenade Damage

    • Good luck in your Operation Riptide challenges this week!
