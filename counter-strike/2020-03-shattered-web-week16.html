Operation Shattered Web
Week 16 Challenges

Grady Hooker (@GradyHooker)

2020.03.03

"Time To Take Out The Trash" - Finally after 16 weeks of waiting, Valve give the players another co-op mission. This time you must destroy a Phoenix production facility and eliminate Franz Kriegeld. You can also get the Diamond Coin now if you get all possible stars.

Shattered Web Week 16 Challenges:

  • Feather Dusting (1 Star): Massacre 20 chickens in Deathmatch: Defusal Group Delta
  • Oil Spill (1 Star): Blow up 10 explosive barrels in Danger Zone
  • Shatter Expectations (2 Stars): Break 200 panes of glass in Casual: Hostage Group
  • Reduce, Reuse, Recycle (2 Stars): Get 10 round wins in Competitive: Agency
  • Great Location, Some Explosions (3 Stars): Get 16 round wins in Wingman: Lake
  • Quarantined, Isolated, Eliminated (3 Stars): Destroy the Phoenix production facility and eliminate Franz Kriegeld in Phoenix Facility

    • Good luck in your Shattered Web challenges this week!
