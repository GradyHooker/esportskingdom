Week 16 Challenges

Grady Hooker (@GradyHooker)

2021.03.16

"Ancient Danger" - The last week of missions and that means it is time to wrap it up with another co-op mission and a whole lot of action on the map Ancient including Guardian and Competitive.

Broken Fang Week 16 Challenges:

  • Just Scratching The Surface: Win rounds in Competitive: Ancient (7 rounds, 15 rounds, 20 rounds)
  • That Belong In A Museum! (2 stars): Get 25 UMP-45 kills in Guardian: Engage
  • Lost And Found: Win rounds in Retakes (3 rounds, 7 rounds, 10 rounds)
  • Keep Your Eyes Shut!: Blind the enemy with flashbangs in Casual: Defusal Group Sigma (7 enemies, 15 enemies, 25 enemies)
  • A Lasting Impression (2 stars): ...the retina quickly adapts to the bright stimulus, creating temporary blindness

    • Good luck in your Broken Fang challenges this week!
